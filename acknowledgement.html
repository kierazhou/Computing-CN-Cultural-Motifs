Honoring the research, media, and mentorship that made this archive possible.

Special Thanks

Building Mapping Chinese Cultural Motifs has been a collaborative and generative experience. I would like to express my deepest gratitude to my practicum advisors and faculty mentors for their invaluable feedback, theoretical guidance, and ongoing support throughout the development of this project.

A special acknowledgement is owed to the creators of the Culturally Situated Design Tools (CSDT) framework, whose foundational work in ethnocomputing inspired the pedagogical approach of this archive. This project builds upon their mission to weave computational thinking together with cultural responsiveness.

    Historical context on Cloud Collars referenced from HanGrace / NewHanfu.
