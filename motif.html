The Roots of the Cloud

🏛️
Archival Photo
Sepia-toned historical loom

Sichuan brocade, or Shu brocade, is one of the four famous brocades in China, with a history stretching back over 2,000 years. The cloud motif is not merely decorative; it represents the breath of the earth and the connection between the mortal realm and the heavens.

This section directly addresses the intersection of traditional weaving techniques and algorithmic thinking long before the invention of modern computers, challenging the notion that technical rigor is devoid of deep creative history.
